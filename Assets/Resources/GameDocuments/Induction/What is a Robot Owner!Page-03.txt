<b>ESCAPE POLICY</b>

The policy on prisoners attempting to escape from other penitentiary companies is often harsh and unforgiving. 

Our policy is, we believe, form but fair. Prisoners who attempt to escape are put out as bounties for People Hunters to track them down. Instead of the "dead or alive" option, we elect for "only alive" and the prisoner's time-served figure is started again (as if they had just been sentenced).

For you reference, no-one has ever escaped/
